Overview

Welcome to the Web Development Program! Whether you're joining us online or in person, we're excited to embark on this journey with you. In Level 1, you'll be introduced to fundamental concepts that form the backbone of web development. These include HTML, CSS, and JS, more specifically Javascript functionality. Level 1 focuses on Loops and Logic. This is the concept of looping through our data in some way and providing logic to that data. (For Loops/ While Loops/ If Else statements) By mastering these concepts, you'll build a strong foundation that will serve you well throughout your web development journey.

As you progress through Level 1, don't forget to check out our live demo of the Capstone project you'll be building for your final project. This project will encapsulate everything you've learned and allow you to showcase your skills as a budding web developer.

Coursework

Complete the coursework. These lessons and assignments are meant to be completed in a specific order to teach you the right amount of material at the right times. Some students lament that they didn't know CSS Flex and CSS Grid earlier, so some skipping around may be acceptable.

Level 1 is a journey! You'll do best if you make friends, tire yourself out on the journey every day, and take time to appreciate how far you've come along the way. If you're on it, you'll have found a crew and you'll be ready for the next adventure, and it will only take a few weeks.
